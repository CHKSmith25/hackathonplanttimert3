The Pomodoro Technique

The pomodoro technique a time-management tool which splits your working time into 25 minute intervals, called "pomodoros", with 5 minute breaks in between.

Click the start button to set your timer and after each successful pomodoro, you'll see your desk plant grow. Once you've completed 2 successful pomodoros, your plant will have fully grown and you'll be able to add it to your window sill. After this, enjoy the view and a nice break, and then start growing your next plant!
